Currently: Senior Designer / Art Director at Bandier

My name is Estefi Panizza, I’m a senior graphic designer & art director living and working in East London.

I’ve always been fascinated by beautiful things - the love and passion that goes into making them.

I specialise in branding, art direction and web design. I have more than 4 years experience working for the fashion, wellness and high end industries. My passion is creating powerful brand experiences from the ground up. My design style is inspired by contemporary art, fashion and music.

I partner with women to bring their stories and brands to life. Through a combination of strategic thinking and the creation of memorable design, I work with you to help grow the business and bring design that is beautiful, brave and exceeds expectations.
