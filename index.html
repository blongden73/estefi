My name is Estefi Panizza, I’m a senior graphic designer & art director living and working in East London.

I’ve always been fascinated by beautiful things - the love and passion that goes into making them.

I specialise in branding, art direction and web design. I have more than 4 years experience working for the fashion, wellness and high end industries. My passion is creating powerful brand experiences from the ground up. My design style is inspired by contemporary art, fashion and music.

I partner with women to bring their stories and brands to life, through a combination of strategic thinking and the creation of memorable design. I'd love to work with you to help you grow the business and create design that is beautiful, brave and exceeds expectations.

BANDIER x Sustainable

For the launch of the BANDIER Sustainability Edit, I designed & developed an identity to run across all campaign content. The identity aims to reflect the ethos of sustainable activewear and the launch of a capsule collection made from recycled yarn. The design looks at how technology can be used in a positive way to help with sustainability. I wanted the design to bring a bold and bright look, steering away from the traditional view that sustainble needs to be earthy and plain. — Campaign Launch, Art Direction, Graphic Design
