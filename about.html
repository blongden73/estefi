About
I’m a Senior Art Director with 10+ years of experience across fashion, beauty, and high- end lifestyle brands. With a foundation in graphic design and marketing psychology, my work is rooted in thoughtful story telling — creating images and ideas that feel intentional, modern, and quietly luxurious.

I lead with concept, craft, and intuition. My approach is collaborative and deeply considered, shaped by an enduring love of fashion, culture, and the way design can make people feel. I aim for work that is refined yet expressive, where every detail has purpose and every touchpoint reflects a sense of ease and sophistication.
