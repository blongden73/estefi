My name is Estefi Panizza, I’m a senior graphic designer & art director living and working in East London.

I’ve always been fascinated by beautiful things - the love and passion that goes into making them.

I specialise in branding, art direction and web design. I have more than 5 years experience working for the fashion, wellness and high end industries. My passion is creating powerful brand experiences from the ground up. My design style is inspired by contemporary art, fashion and music.

I partner with brands to bring their stories to life, through a combination of strategic thinking and the creation of memorable design. I'd love to work with you to help you grow the business and create design that is beautiful, brave and exceeds expectations.
